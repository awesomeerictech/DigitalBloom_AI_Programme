# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## About the Programme

**What is DigitalBloom?**
DigitalBloom is a comprehensive AI capacity building programme delivered by Stratetactical Solutions Limited (STL). It covers 30 courses across 11 thematic areas, from AI fundamentals to advanced agentic engineering, with a strong community programme for underserved groups.

**Do I need a technical background to join?**
No. Most DigitalBloom courses are designed for non-technical learners. The programme starts from absolute basics and progresses to technical tracks for those who want to go deeper. The community programme is specifically designed for people with no prior AI experience.

**What language is the programme delivered in?**
Primarily English, with Swahili support available for community cohorts in Kenya. We are working to expand to additional local languages. Contact us if your community requires a specific language.

**How long does the programme take?**
It depends on which level and how many series you complete. A single series is 5 sessions × 3 hours = 15 hours. The Foundation Certificate typically requires 1–3 series. The full Specialist pathway can span 150–300 hours across multiple courses.

## For Community Groups

**Is the community programme really free?**
For eligible community groups with donor funding available — yes. The pilot session is always free for everyone. Full programme access depends on available funding, which is why donor support is so important.

**How do we apply for a community programme?**
Email stratetacticallimited@gmail.com with your organisation's name, location, number of participants, and a brief description of your community. We respond within 5 working days.

**What if we cannot access the internet for training?**
We have delivered sessions in low-connectivity environments. Contact us and we will design a delivery approach appropriate for your context, including potential data or device support where funding allows.

**Can a church or mosque request training for its congregation?**
Absolutely. Faith communities are a priority group for the DigitalBloom community programme. We have experience working with faith-based organisations and can deliver content in a manner consistent with your community's values.

## For Donors & Supporters

**How do I know my donation reaches the community?**
All donors receive an impact report showing how their funds were used, how many participants were reached, and what outcomes were achieved. For larger donations, we provide detailed narrative and financial reporting.

**Can I direct my donation to a specific community or programme?**
Yes. If you want to fund training for a specific community, geography, demographic, or course type, contact us at stratetacticallimited@gmail.com and we will set up a directed grant.

**Is DigitalBloom a registered organisation?**
DigitalBloom is a programme of **Stratetactical Solutions Limited (STL)**, a registered company in Kenya providing Professional, Scientific and Technical Services.

**Can I get a receipt for my donation?**
Yes. For all bank and M-PESA contributions, email us after your transfer and we will issue a formal receipt. PayPal transactions generate automatic receipts via PayPal.

**What is the minimum donation amount?**
There is no minimum. Every contribution, however small, is welcomed and acknowledged. Even KES 500 contributes to training materials for a participant.

## For Institutions

**What does the institution need to provide?**
Primarily a physical or virtual learning space, support with learner mobilisation, and scheduling/admin coordination. STL handles everything related to curriculum, facilitation, quality, and certification.

**Do you offer a trial before we commit?**
Yes — always. Our FCRM commitment means every institution receives a free pilot session before any agreement is signed. We believe you should experience the quality before you invest.

**Can the programme be co-branded with our institution?**
Yes. We offer co-branding opportunities for institutional and corporate partners, subject to a partnership agreement. Your institution's brand can appear alongside DigitalBloom on certificates and programme materials.

**Is there a minimum cohort size?**
We recommend a minimum of 15 participants per cohort for cost-effectiveness, but we can accommodate smaller groups depending on context and availability.

## Certification & Credentials

**Are DigitalBloom certificates recognised by employers?**
DigitalBloom certificates are industry-aligned credentials with verifiable digital badges. Employer recognition grows as more organisations see DigitalBloom graduates performing at a high level with AI tools. We are actively building employer partnerships to strengthen recognition.

**Can I share my certificate on LinkedIn?**
Yes. All certificates come with a digital badge in Open Badge format that you can upload directly to your LinkedIn profile.

**What happens if I miss a session?**
Contact your facilitator as soon as possible. Make-up arrangements are handled on a case-by-case basis. Missed sessions may delay certification depending on the assessment requirements of your course level.
