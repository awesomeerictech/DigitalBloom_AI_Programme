## Cross-Cutting Component: Prompt Engineering

Prompt Engineering is not a standalone course — it is **embedded across all 30 courses** at three levels:

| Level | Focus | Where Embedded |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 **Basic** | Writing clear, effective prompts for common tasks | All Foundation-level sessions |
| 🔵 **Intermediate** | Role prompting, chain-of-thought, few-shot examples, output formatting | Professional-level sessions |
| 🟣 **Advanced** | System prompts, agentic pipelines, multi-step reasoning, API-level prompting | Expert and Specialist sessions |
