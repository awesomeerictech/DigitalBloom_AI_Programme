# 👩‍🏫 DigitalBloom Facilitators

> *DigitalBloom is only as good as the people who deliver it. We are always looking for passionate, qualified AI practitioners who want to use their skills to serve communities.*

---

## Who is a DigitalBloom Facilitator?

A DigitalBloom facilitator is a qualified AI practitioner who delivers training sessions to community, institutional, and professional cohorts. Facilitators are the human face of the programme — they translate complex AI concepts into practical, accessible skills that participants can use from Day 1.

---

## What Facilitators Do

- Deliver DigitalBloom sessions (virtual, in-person, or hybrid)
- Adapt curriculum content to the specific context and needs of each cohort
- Administer assessments and provide feedback to participants
- Collect participant feedback as part of the FCRM process
- Contribute to curriculum improvement based on delivery experience
- (For community facilitators) Train local volunteers to extend programme reach

---

## Facilitator Profiles

### 🌟 Lead Facilitators (STL Staff)
Full-time or contracted AI practitioners employed or retained by STL to deliver the programme. Lead Facilitators are responsible for quality assurance and curriculum integrity.

### 🌿 Associate Facilitators
Qualified practitioners who deliver DigitalBloom sessions on a sessional or contract basis. Associate Facilitators are vetted, trained, and quality-monitored by STL.

### 🌱 Community Facilitators
Participants who complete the full DigitalBloom programme and are trained to deliver entry-level sessions to their own communities. Community Facilitators extend the programme's reach at low cost and with high contextual relevance.

---

## Minimum Requirements (Associate Facilitators)

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| 🧠 **AI Knowledge** | Demonstrated working knowledge of major AI tools and concepts |
| 💬 **Communication** | Strong facilitation and presentation skills |
| 🌍 **Community Sensitivity** | Ability to work respectfully and effectively with diverse communities |
| 📋 **Reliability** | Commitment to scheduled sessions, preparation standards, and reporting |
| 🎓 **Preferred** | A DigitalBloom Professional Certificate or equivalent qualification |

---

## Facilitator Benefits

- Paid facilitation fees for commercial programme delivery
- Access to the full DigitalBloom curriculum and resource library
- STL facilitator training and continuous professional development
- Recognition and co-branding in programme materials
- Part of a growing network of African AI practitioners committed to community impact
- Priority consideration for STL consultancy and project opportunities

6. Any relevant qualifications or portfolio links

## Train as a Community Facilitator

If you have completed a DigitalBloom programme and want to bring it to your community, contact us about our **Community Facilitator Development Track** — a short programme that equips you to deliver entry-level DigitalBloom sessions in your own context.

Community Facilitator training is **free** for participants from donor-supported cohorts.
