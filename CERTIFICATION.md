# 🏅 DigitalBloom AI Certification Framework

> A structured progression pathway enabling learners to advance from foundational AI knowledge to specialised expertise — with verifiable, industry-aligned credentials at every stage.

---

## Certification Pathway Overview

```
🟢 FOUNDATION          🔵 PROFESSIONAL         🟣 EXPERT              🏆 SPECIALIST
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AI Literacy &          Applied AI in           Advanced AI            Agentic Systems &
Core Skills            Business Functions      Systems & Strategy     Innovation Leadership

DigitalBloom           DigitalBloom            DigitalBloom           DigitalBloom
AI Foundation          AI Professional         AI Expert              AI Specialist
Certificate            Certificate             Certificate            Certificate
```

All learners start at Foundation and progress through each level. Experienced learners may fast-track based on prior experience or assessment.

---

## Level 1: Foundation Certificate

**DigitalBloom AI Foundation Certificate**

| | |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Build a strong understanding of AI fundamentals and everyday applications |
| **Duration** | 1–3 series (15–45 hours) |
| **Target Learners** | Beginners, community members, non-technical professionals, anyone new to AI |

### Key Competencies
- AI concepts and terminology
- AI tools for personal and professional productivity
- Basic prompt engineering
- Introductory automation with AI tools
- Responsible and ethical AI use at the basic level

### Aligned Courses
- Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence: From Theory to Practice
- AI Tools Training *(Community Gateway Course)*
- Business Writing in the Age of AI Masterclass
- AI Essentials for Office Managers and Secretaries
- Emotional Intelligence in the AI Era

### Assessment
- Practical assignments and tool-based exercises
- Short implementation project
- Facilitator observation rubric

## Level 2: Professional Certificate

**DigitalBloom AI Professional Certificate**

| | |
|---|---|

| **Duration** | 3–6 series (45–90 hours) |
| **Target Learners** | Working professionals, managers, HR, finance, communications, and marketing practitioners |

### Key Competencies
- Workflow automation for specific professional roles
- Role-based AI implementation and tool selection
- Data-driven decision-making with AI
- Intermediate prompt engineering
- AI-powered communication, marketing, and business execution

### Aligned Courses
- AI for Leaders and Managers
- AI in Accounting and Finance
- AI in Human Resource Management
- AI for Procurement and Supply Chain Excellence
- AI-Powered Marketing and Customer Relationship Management
- AI-Powered Career Management
- AI-Powered PR/Journalism/Communications and Branding Mastery

### Assessment
- Role-specific case studies
- AI implementation project submitted as a professional deliverable

## Level 4: Specialist Certificate

**DigitalBloom AI Specialist Certificate**

| | |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Achieve mastery in specialised AI domains and lead innovation |
| **Duration** | 10–20 series (150–300 hours) |
| **Target Learners** | AI engineers, senior technical leaders, innovation champions, advanced practitioners |

### Key Competencies
- Agentic AI system design and deployment
- End-to-end AI solution development
- Ethical AI governance at the organisational level
- Cross-domain AI integration (technical + business + communication)
- Innovation leadership and AI scalability

### Aligned Courses
- AI-Powered Agentic Engineering
- AI-Powered Agentic AI Engineering Specialization
- AI Ethics and Guardrails
- AI-Powered Project Management
- Specialised tracks within Marketing, PR, and Career Management (advanced series)

### Assessment
- **Capstone project** — end-to-end AI solution built and documented
- **Hackathon or innovation challenge** participation
- **Industry-impact implementation** — demonstrable real-world deployment

## Certificate Features

All DigitalBloom certificates include:

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| 📜 **Digital Certificate** | PDF certificate with unique ID, learner name, course details, and date |
| 🏷️ **Verifiable Badge** | Open Badge standard digital badge shareable on LinkedIn, CV, and email |
| 📁 **Portfolio Evidence** | Capstone project portfolio included as evidence of competence |
| ✅ **Competency Validation** | Industry-aligned skills mapped to real-world job requirements |
| 🔄 **Continuous Learning Path** | Clear pathway from each certificate to the next level |
