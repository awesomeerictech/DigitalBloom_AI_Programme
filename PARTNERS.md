# 🤝 DigitalBloom Partners & Collaboration

> We believe the AI literacy challenge is too large for any single organisation to solve alone. DigitalBloom is built for partnership.

---

## Why Partner with DigitalBloom?

DigitalBloom offers partners a **ready-to-deploy, quality-assured AI training ecosystem** — complete with curriculum, facilitators, certification, and quality systems. Partners do not need to build AI training capability from scratch. We bring it to you.

Partnering with DigitalBloom means:
- 🌸 Instant access to 30 professionally developed AI courses
- 🎓 Certified learning outcomes for your beneficiaries or staff
- 🧩 Flexibility to co-brand and adapt within the DigitalBloom framework
- 📊 Documented impact data for your own reporting and stakeholder communication
- 🤝 A collaborative, not transactional, relationship with STL

---

## Partnership Models

### 🏛️ Institutional / Academic Partners

**For:** Universities, colleges, TVET institutions, training centres, schools

| What You Provide | What STL Delivers |
|---|---|
| Physical or virtual learning space | Full curriculum design and materials |
| Learner mobilisation and enrolment | Qualified AI practitioner facilitators |
| Scheduling and admin coordination | All training delivery and quality assurance |
| Institutional endorsement | DigitalBloom certification for your learners |

> Institutions receive free pilot sessions before signing any agreement.

---

### 🌍 Development Agency & NGO Partners

**For:** UN agencies, international NGOs, bilateral development programmes, regional bodies

| Partnership Form | Description |
|---|---|
| **Programme Grant** | Fund delivery of DigitalBloom for a defined community cohort or demographic |
| **Technical Partnership** | Integrate DigitalBloom into an existing digital skills or livelihoods programme |
| **Research Collaboration** | Partner on impact research, needs assessment, or curriculum development |
| **Scale Partnership** | Fund geographic expansion into new regions or countries |

---

### 🏢 Corporate Partners (CSR / ESG)

**For:** Companies seeking to invest community AI literacy as part of their social responsibility mandate

| Tier | Description |
|---|---|
| **Community Sponsor** | Fund free sessions for a defined community in your operational area |
| **Skills Partner** | Fund training for your supply chain community, vendor SMEs, or local partners |
| **Employee Programme** | Commission DigitalBloom for your own staff as a professional development benefit |
| **Naming Sponsor** | Fund and co-brand a full community programme track |

Corporate partners receive:
- Co-branding on all funded programme materials
- Impact report for ESG/CSR disclosure
- Media acknowledgement (with consent)
- Certificate of Partnership from STL

---

### 🏛️ Government Partners

**For:** National and county governments, public sector bodies, regulatory agencies

| Partnership Area | Description |
|---|---|
| **Civil Servant Upskilling** | Deliver DigitalBloom to government staff as a digital transformation initiative |
| **Community Development** | Integrate DigitalBloom into county community development or youth programmes |
| **Education Integration** | Work with the Ministry of Education to embed AI literacy in school or TVET curricula |
| **Policy Development** | Collaborate with STL on AI governance frameworks for public sector application |

---

### ⛪ Faith-Based Organisation Partners

**For:** Churches, mosques, religious networks, faith-based development organisations

Faith communities have extraordinary reach — and their members deserve AI literacy as much as anyone. DigitalBloom works with FBOs to:

- Deliver AI training for congregation members in a culturally appropriate context
- Train church/mosque administrative and leadership teams
- Support FBO-run schools, clinics, and community services with AI tools
- Provide content sensitive to faith community values, including our AI Ethics module

---

### 📡 Technology Partners

**For:** AI tool companies, tech platforms, internet providers, device manufacturers

| Partnership Form | Description |
|---|---|
| **Tool Access Sponsorship** | Provide free or subsidised AI tool access for community learners |
| **Connectivity Support** | Fund data bundles or hotspot access for low-connectivity community sessions |
| **Device Partnership** | Provide tablets or laptops for device-poor community cohorts |
| **Platform Integration** | Feature DigitalBloom curriculum within your own learning platform |

Technology partners are acknowledged in all programme materials and receive brand visibility with the communities we serve.
