# 💰 Support DigitalBloom

> **DigitalBloom is open in spirit and community-driven.**
> Every contribution — from a single community member's data bundle to a foundation grant — directly funds AI literacy for people who need it most.

---

## Why Your Support Matters

Artificial Intelligence is reshaping every sector — healthcare, finance, education, agriculture, governance, and media. Communities and organisations that are not equipped to participate in this transformation will be left behind — not because they lack capacity, but because they lack access to quality training.

**DigitalBloom exists to change that.**

We run the programme commercially for institutions that can sustain it, and at **little or no cost** for communities, grassroots groups, youth, women's organisations, faith communities, and underserved populations. But free delivery is only possible with donor and sponsor support.

Your contribution is not charity — it is an **investment in human capital, community resilience, and equitable digital transformation.**

---

## What Your Support Funds

| Area | Detail |
|---|---|
| 🖥️ **Facilitator Time** | Each free community session requires 6–10 hours of expert preparation, delivery, and post-session support. Your support pays for qualified AI practitioners to give their best. |
| 📦 **Training Materials** | Participant workbooks, prompt engineering guides, tool access reference sheets, and printed handouts for learners without reliable internet. |
| 🌐 **AI Tool Subscriptions** | Premium AI tools (Synthesia, Midjourney, Jasper, Notion AI, and others) are provided to participants during sessions. These have real subscription costs that support covers. |
| 🏫 **Venue & Logistics** | Transport, room setup, power/internet provision, and refreshments for in-person community sessions — especially in rural or low-income settings. |
| 🌍 **Geographic Expansion** | Scaling DigitalBloom to new communities, counties, and countries requires investment in facilitator recruitment, training, and deployment. |
| 🌸 **Curriculum Development** | AI is the fastest-moving field in the world. Keeping 30 courses current and relevant requires continuous research, development, and expert review. |
| 📜 **Free Certifications** | Every community learner deserves a verifiable credential for their achievement. Support covers the issuance of digital certificates and badges at no cost to participants. |
| 👩‍🏫 **Facilitator Development** | Training local community members to become DigitalBloom facilitators multiplies impact — one trained facilitator can serve hundreds of learners. |
| 🗣️ **Local Language Adaptation** | Contextualising and translating content for Swahili-speaking and other non-English communities makes the programme genuinely accessible. |
| 📡 **Connectivity Support** | Providing data bundles, hotspots, or device access for participants in low-connectivity or low-income areas removes the last barrier to participation. |
| 🔬 **Impact Research** | Documenting, measuring, and publishing the outcomes of community training builds the evidence base for continued funding and programme improvement. |

## Partnership Models for Organisations

Beyond direct financial support, DigitalBloom welcomes the following partnership models:

| Model | Description |
|---|---|
| 🏛️ **Institutional Grant** | Foundations and development agencies fund cohort delivery for specific communities or demographics |
| 🤝 **Co-Implementation** | Partner organisations provide venue, logistics, and learner mobilisation; STL delivers the curriculum |
| 🏢 **Corporate Social Responsibility** | Companies fund community AI training as part of their CSR/ESG commitments — with co-branding |
| 🌍 **Government Partnership** | County and national government units commission DigitalBloom for civil servants and communities |
| ⛪ **Faith Community Sponsorship** | Churches, mosques, and faith networks fund training for their congregations and surrounding communities |
| 📡 **Technology Partner** | Tech companies provide tool access, devices, or connectivity in exchange for programme recognition |
| 🎓 **Academic Partner** | Universities and colleges host DigitalBloom community cohorts on their campuses at no facility cost |
