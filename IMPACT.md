# 📊 DigitalBloom Impact

> *This page tracks the reach, outcomes, and stories of the DigitalBloom community programme. We update it regularly as new cohorts complete training and share their experiences.*

---

## Why Impact Matters to Us

Every contribution to DigitalBloom — whether financial, in-kind, or through partnership — creates real change in real people's lives. We document our impact honestly and transparently because our donors, partners, and communities deserve to know what their investment achieves.

We measure what matters: not just participation numbers, but **changed capabilities, changed opportunities, and changed lives.**

---

## Impact Framework

We track impact across five dimensions:

| Dimension | What We Measure |
|---|---|
| 📣 **Reach** | Number of participants, sessions, cohorts, communities, and geographies served |
| 🎓 **Learning** | Knowledge and skill gains measured through pre/post assessments and practical deliverables |
| 💼 **Application** | How participants use AI skills in their roles, organisations, and daily lives after training |
| 🌱 **Ripple Effect** | How many additional people participants teach, train, or influence after completing DigitalBloom |
| 💰 **Economic Outcome** | Employment gains, income changes, productivity improvements, and cost savings attributable to AI skills |

## Impact by Sector

### 🎓 Education
- Students in secondary schools and universities gain AI literacy that improves their academic work and employability
- Faculty learn to integrate AI into teaching, research, and curriculum development
- Institutions offer AI-certified skills as a differentiator in competitive higher education markets

### ⛪ Faith Communities
- Church and mosque administrators use AI to streamline operations, communication, and outreach
- Faith-based schools integrate AI tools into classroom teaching
- Community development arms of FBOs use AI for project reporting and donor communication

### 🏘️ Community & Civil Society
- CBOs use AI to write better proposals, track programmes, and report to donors
- Community leaders communicate more effectively with their constituencies
- Women's groups access AI career tools to improve livelihoods and entrepreneurship

### 🏛️ Local Government
- Ward and county officers use AI for data management, citizen communication, and service delivery reporting
- Local planning processes benefit from AI-assisted data analysis and community consultation tools

### 💼 Individual Economic Empowerment
- Job seekers use AI career management tools to improve employment outcomes
- Freelancers build AI-powered services and increase income
- Entrepreneurs use AI tools for marketing, customer management, and business planning

## Donor Impact Reporting

All donors who support DigitalBloom receive:

- **Immediate acknowledgement** upon receipt of contribution
- **Programme report** showing how their funds were deployed
- **Beneficiary data** — participant numbers, demographics, and completion rates
- **Outcome summary** — what participants learned and how they plan to apply it
- **Stories** — with participant consent, qualitative impact stories from funded cohorts
- **Certificate of Impact** — formal documentation of the community impact their funding created

For institutional donors and foundations, we provide full narrative and financial reporting aligned with your grant requirements.
